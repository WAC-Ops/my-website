A Company Built to Last

William Allen Company was founded with a simple belief: that businesses deserve a fastener partner they can genuinely rely on. From day one, our mission has been to deliver accurate specifications, competitive pricing, and responsive service to manufacturers and contractors across the country.

As a proudly American owned and operated company, we take seriously our responsibility to support the growth of our economy and the industries we serve. Every order we fulfill is backed by a team that cares about getting it right — not just this time, but every time.

What started as a focused wholesale operation has grown into a trusted resource for businesses of all sizes. Whether you need a standard bolt or a hard-to-find specialty part, we bring the same dedication to every inquiry — because no order is too small, and no specification is too complex.
